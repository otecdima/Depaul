The Story of the club

In order to share their love of movies with others, a group of people established Moviefans in 2022. We are the place with events and activities happening all year long that are based in Ukraine. We like to maintain the enthusiasm and excitement while continually inviting new participants in our club. We simply enjoy spending time together doing the things we like the most.
