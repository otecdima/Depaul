How to Tie a Shoe

by Dmytro Batko

Step 1

First step is to make a knot like on the photo.

Step 2

Pull two ends of the shoelaces to tight that knot.

Step 3

Make the shoelaces with loops like on the photo.

Step 4

Make the knot like at the start of the process with that two shoelaces from the previous step.

Step 5

Pull those two shoelaces to tight our knot.
Here you are, the knot is ready and shoes are tied.
